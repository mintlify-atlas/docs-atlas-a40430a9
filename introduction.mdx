# Welcome to EnvaSistema

EnvaSistema is a mobile inventory management system designed for warehouse operations at Enva Enterprise. Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose for Android, it streamlines inventory tracking, transfers, and transformations with integrated barcode scanning capabilities.

## Overview

EnvaSistema provides a comprehensive solution for managing warehouse inventory operations on mobile devices. The application enables warehouse operators to efficiently track inbound and outbound inventory movements, perform transfers between locations, and manage package assembly and disassembly operations.

## Who is this for?

EnvaSistema is designed for:

- **Warehouse Operators** - Front-line staff managing daily inventory operations
- **Shift Supervisors** - Overseeing warehouse activities and tracking performance
- **Inventory Managers** - Monitoring stock levels and movement patterns
- **Operations Teams** - Coordinating warehouse logistics and workflows

## Technology Stack

- **Platform**: Android (minSdk 33, targetSdk 35)
- **Language**: Kotlin
- **UI Framework**: Jetpack Compose with Material 3
- **Navigation**: Jetpack Navigation Compose
- **Build System**: Gradle with Kotlin DSL
